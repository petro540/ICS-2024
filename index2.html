SECRET STORY ABOUT KIZUKA

KIZUKA (TPDF) HIGH SCHOOL is found in Morogoro region. Our school is one of the best schools found in Morogoro due to its good performance, in different issues. Kizuka has been blessed with good environment, and attractive for learning, also it has got good teachers who teach students very well and they understand what they are taught.

Our school had been having different events of sports and games, where by the participants went to Iringa for the games, was to create good relationship with other school .kizuka was announced to be the winner. Of those games and it was awarded with medals and gifts, for their good work and discipline that they showed.
